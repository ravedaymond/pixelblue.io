Every Case Study will be listed as one of three categories:

  • LAB

    • Experiments or prototypes for scripts, tools, extensions, and the like. Work categorized as LAB will more often have a less detailed case study than JOB or PROJECT work.

  • JOB

    • Any work hired to contribute to or complete by a client. Only work categorized as JOB will display client name and testimonials.

  • PROJECT

    • Any work that is planned for future release under the Pixel Blue brand.

Every Case Study will show a tag for what work was created, revised, or contributed to:

  • CODE

    • If any work was done in any programming or scripting language.

  • DESIGN

    • If any work was done during the design process or planning phases.

  • GRAPHICS

    • If any work was done with any graphics, art, logos, and the like.

Every Case Study will list any collaborators who assisted in any creation, revision, or contribution to the work done.

    • All collaborators will be linked to their preferred website or contact information.

Every Case Study will show received customer testimonial at the very bottom of the study.

    • Testimonials displayed on this webpage will be excerpts of the full testimonial. The full testimonial is available on the PDF download of the respective Case Study.
