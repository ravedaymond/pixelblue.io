What is Pixel Blue?

Originally intended only as an online alias, Pixel Blue was created as a brand for business, publishing, and personal work. The creation of this brand and this webpage sparked a passion for web development and design that was absent prior to.

My hope is to have Pixel Blue represent a clean, minimal style of design while having a very interactive, almost gamified, user experience. Information should be readily available in a very easy to read way, but also engaging to users.

To me, it represents a next step towards starting my career post-graduation with a strong presence in the developer community. It also gives me a firm platform to publish personal projects the first of which is under heavy prototyping at this time.
